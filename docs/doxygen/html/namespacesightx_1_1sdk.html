UYVY

YUY2 

4:2:2 YUV format, also known as YUYV, YUNV; 16 bpp; like UYVY with U and V reversed

IYUV 

4:2:0 YUV format, also known as I420; 12 bpp; 8-bit Y plane followed by 8-bit subsampled U and V planes

YV12 

4:2:0 YUV format, also known as 420p; 12 bpp; like IYUV with U and V reversed

NV12 

4:2:0 YUV format; 12 bpp; 8-bit Y plane followed by interleaved U/V plane

NV21 

4:2:0 YUV format, also known as 420sp; 12 bpp; like NV12 with U and V reversed

Compressed 

one of OpenCV supported codecs
